Welcome to my (non-academic) Homepage


I have been teaching computer science for thirty years, mainly software engineering, programming languages (e.g. C, C++, Java, Python) and Unix system programming. Now, I work as a lecturer at the Ensicaen (a french national graduate school of engineering).

My research activity initially focused on algorithms for image processing and analysis, mainly in the medical field. It now extends to the modeling of complex systems such as the understanding of biological and physiological mechanisms.

My other interests are in the areas of biology, ancient and medieval history, and geopolitics.
